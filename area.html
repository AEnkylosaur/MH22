Area Formula Guide

Rectangle

Multiply the length by the width.

Square

Multiply one side by itself.

Triangle

Multiply the base and height, then divide by two.

Circle

Multiply π by the square of the radius.

Parallelogram

Multiply the base by the height.

Trapezoid

Add the two bases, multiply by the height, then divide by two.
