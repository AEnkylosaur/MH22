Perimeter Formula Guide

Rectangle

Add the length and width, then multiply the result by two.

Square

Multiply one side by four.

Triangle

Add all three side lengths together.

Circle

The perimeter of a circle is called its circumference.

Parallelogram

Add the base and side length, then multiply by two.
