Volume Formula Guide

Cube

Raise the side length to the third power.

Cylinder

Multiply π, the square of the radius, and the height.

Cone

Take one-third of the cylinder volume with the same base and height.

Sphere

Multiply four-thirds of π by the cube of the radius.

Rectangular Prism

Multiply the length, width, and height.
